The spring air is filled with the sweet scent of blooming flora! It is a magical time at Kordiak Park, and we've seen a wonderful variety of life returning to our trails.

### What to Look For:
* **Trillium**: These delicate white flowers are appearing in the shaded areas.
* **Wild Violets**: Look closely near the base of the older oaks.
* **Dandelions**: Bright yellow pops of color are appearing in the open clearings.

> "Nature does not hurry, yet everything is accomplished." — *Lao Tzu*

Next time you're out for a walk, take a moment to stop and appreciate these small, beautiful details. Happy flower watching!
